Dukes Cricket Fantasy Format

  • Every Dukes club member is eligible and invited to participate in the 2014 Fantasy Cricket. The following is all you need to do to participate.
    1. Select 7 players every week after the playing 11 is announced based on who you think will perform the best.
    2. Selection Criteria is you need to have minimum 2 Batsmen, 2 Bowlers and 1 All-Rounder in your team. 6th and 7th roster spots are flex players. He can be either a batsmen, bowler, all-rounder or a keeper.
    3. Name your Team.
    4. Identify one of your playing 7 in your team as a "PowerPlayer" (PP). This person will score double points for you.Note: You cannot pick the same Power Player for two consecutive weeks.
    5. Submit your Team to Dukes website by Friday 11:59 pm every week. No teams will be entertained after the cut off day and time.
    6. Root for/Encourage/Bribe the players you selected as part of your Dream Team and hope (J) they perform well for you, LOL

Scoring Categories - Batting

  • Each run - 2 points.
  • Bonus
    • Not Out (10 runs minimum) - 10 points
    • 15 runs - 15 points.
    • 25 runs - 25 points.
    • 50 runs - 50 points.
    • 75 runs - 75 points.
    • 100+ - 100 points. (Special treat by Dukes Management :-) )
    • SRR Bonus:
      • 75 to 94.99 Strike Rate (after scoring 10 runs) - 15 points
      • 95 to 114.99 Strike Rate (after scoring 10 runs) - 30 points
      • 115+ Strike Rate (after scoring 10 runs) - 50 points

Scoring Categories - Bowling

  • Each wicket - 20 points.
  • Each maiden over - 10 points.
  • Bonus
    • 2 wickets - 15 points.
    • 3-4 wickets - 30 points.
    • 5 wickets - 50 points.
    • 6 wickets - 75 points.
    • 7+ wickets - 100 points
    • No extras - 20 points
    • Economy rate bonus:
      • Between 0.00 and 2.50 runs per over - 50 points
      • Between 2.51 and 3.50 runs per over - 30 points

Scoring Categories - Fielding

  • Each catch - 20 points.
  • Each stumping - 20 points.
  • Each runout - 10 points.

Scoring Categories - MOM

  • MOM player - 50 points.

Scoring Categories - Weekly Fantasy Winners

  • 1st Place: 100 points
  • 2nd Place: 75 points
  • 3rd Place: 50 points
